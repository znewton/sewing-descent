Frequently Asked Questions

My waxed canvas gear got dirty. How do I clean it?

Don't throw it in wash! High temps and detergent will ruin the wax coating, and you'll have to fully re-wax it.

To properly clean your waxed canvas gear, in increasing order of grime stubbornness:

  1. Wipe dirt away with a dry towel or natural bristle brush.
  2. Hose it down or wipe with a wet towel.
  3. Spot-clean a particularly dirty area with isopropyl alcohol. This might require re-waxing the cleaned spot.

"Sewing Descent?" That doesn't make sense... What does the name mean?

"Sewing Descent" is a bit of fun wordplay on the phrase "sow dissent."

“Sow Dissent”

To attempt to cause disagreement by subtle means.
— Your Dictionary

"Sow[ing]" is replaced by "Sewing" because that's what we do: we sew gear! "Dissent" is replaced by "Descent", alluding to the rewarding activity of descending mountains (whether hiking, biking, skiing, etc.). Lastly, we chose the phrase "Sowing Dissent" because we are "attempting to cause disagreement [with capitalism and consumerism] by subtle means!"
