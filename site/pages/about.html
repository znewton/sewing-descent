About

Sewing Descent is a hobby project created by Zach Newton to make outdoor gear that combines ultralight design principles with sustainable materials.

Materials

Our goal is to use avoid petroleum-based materials wherever possible. Primarily, we use waxed canvas for external shelll fabric, and factory scraps when thin, lightweight plastic is preferred.

However, sometimes materials that break down quickly can drasticly reduce the durability or longevity of a product. Also, this is just a small, hobby project, so petroleum alternatives can be cost prohibitive or entirely unavailable. In these cases, minimal but intentional use of petroleum products is required.
